Kruger National Park

Orpen gate is approximately 35km from the house this is the closest gate, great for a full-day family adventure
to see the Big 5 in their natural habitat. Park entry fees are R460 per adult and R230 per child under 12.
We also offer the services of our Open Safari Vehicle with a qualified to enhance this experience. This is R2000
for the vehicle and Guide. Entrance fees are not included.
Great tip - pack a picnic basket and stop at a picnic site, hire a skottle and enjoy a self-cooked brunch in the
park

Panorama Route

 The Panorama Route is a scenic route through the Drakensburg Mountain Range which is most famous for its
spectacular views of the landscape and waterfalls. Starting from Hoedspruit the tour is a full-day excursion. We
stop at Bourke’s Luck Potholes, Blyde River Canyon, The Three Rondawels, Berlin Falls, Lisbon Falls, The
Pinnacle, God’s Window, and lastly the small town of Graskop for a late lunch before returning home. All
transport and entrance fees are included in the tour as well as bottled mineral water and your qualified guide.
Call: +27 76 471 0019 | Email: lauren@trophytoyota.com
